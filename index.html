Syntax

Container

grx-row

defines a container holding children side by side

grx-col

defines a container holding children one below the other

Alignment

items-{align1}-{?align2}

aligns the container's children in the horizontal, vertical or both axes.
The directions are identical in both grx-row and grx-col.

Horizontal values:
  • left / center / right
  • between (spreads free space between the children)
  • around (spreads free space between, before and after children)
Vertical values:
  • top / middle / bottom

Examples:
items-right
items-middle
items-left-top
items-right-middle
items-center-middle
center
Shorthand for 
items-center-middle
center
Shorthand for 
items-center-middle
pull-{direction}

Pulls this child and all subsequent as far as possible in the given direction.

Directions:
  • left / right
    only apply to children of grx-row
  • top / bottom
    only apply to children of grx-col
  • start / end
    pulls horizontally or vertically, if in grx-row or grx-col, respectively. Useful if grx direction may change

Sizing

            

                
size-{nominator}-{base}

                

                  

                  
    
                    
  • Fraction: an integer between 1 and a given base
    • 
                    
  • Base: 12 / 10 / 5 etc.
    
                    Grx supports many bases out of the box (12, 10, 8, 5, 3).
    
                      Custom bases can be configured in node_modules/grx/src/_params.scss under $bases
    • 
                  

                  Nominator/Base is the fraction of the container's size the current child will occupy. Example:
                  
size-1-5
 will be 1/5 of the container's size.
